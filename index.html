Traverse City S.U.P!

Get out on the water today

Stand up paddle boarding (SUP) is a fun new twist on water board-sports. It has a similar feel to kayaking, but the vertical nature provides a picture perfect view into the water below you, as if you're walking on water. Paddleboarding can be great exercise or as gentle and relaxing as you want so it's fun for everyone. Ben's Boards is here to offer the best paddleboard rentals in Traverse City. Working with Starboard SUP, we can offer a wide range of board sizes at a great value.
